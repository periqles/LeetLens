# Privacy Policy for LeetLens

**Last updated: January 2025**

## Overview

LeetLens is a browser extension that enhances the FACEIT experience by allowing users to upload CS2 demos to Leetify and view player statistics. This extension is a hobby project and does not operate any servers or collect any user data.

## Data Collection

**LeetLens does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its developers.**

## Data Handling

The extension handles the following data locally on your device:

- **Leetify Authentication Token**: Stored locally in your browser to authenticate with Leetify's services. This token never leaves your browser except when communicating directly with Leetify.
- **User Preferences**: Settings like auto-upload toggle are stored locally in your browser's storage.
- **Cached Player Data**: Temporarily cached in your browser to reduce API calls and improve performance.

## Third-Party Services

LeetLens interacts with the following third-party services:

- **FACEIT** (faceit.com): The extension reads match and player information from FACEIT pages you visit.
- **Leetify** (leetify.com): The extension communicates with Leetify's API to upload demos and fetch player statistics.

Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies:
- [FACEIT Privacy Policy](https://faceit.com/en/privacy)
- [Leetify Privacy Policy](https://leetify.com/privacy)

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using the browser's built-in storage API. No data is sent to any server owned or operated by the extension developers.
