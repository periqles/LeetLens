How to Use

  1. Navigate to any FACEIT match room page
  2. Match Prediction: See the win probability panel at the top of the page
  3. Upload Demos: Click the gradient Upload to Leetify button
  4. View Ratings: Leetify ratings, Premier ranks, and FACEIT ELO appear next to player names
  5. See Details: Click any badge to open detailed stats with Overview and Top Maps tabs
